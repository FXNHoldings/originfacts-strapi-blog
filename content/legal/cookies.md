## 1. What cookies and similar technologies are

Cookies are small files placed on your device when you visit a website. Similar technologies include pixels, tags, scripts, local storage, software development kits, link decoration, affiliate identifiers, and other technologies that store information on, or access information from, your device.

We use the word "cookies" in this policy to refer to cookies and similar technologies.

## 2. Why we use cookies

We use cookies to:

1. make the Website work;
2. remember your settings and cookie choices;
3. protect the Website from fraud, spam, and security threats;
4. understand how visitors use the Website;
5. improve pages, travel content, and comparison tools;
6. measure performance of affiliate links and travel offers;
7. attribute affiliate commissions where you click a travel link and later book or buy from a third-party provider; and
8. support newsletter signup forms and embedded tools.

## 3. Types of cookies we use

### 3.1 Strictly necessary cookies

These cookies are needed for the Website to work. They may support page delivery, security, network management, consent storage, account login, form submission, fraud prevention, and basic functionality.

You cannot usually turn these off through our cookie banner because the Website may not work properly without them. You can block them in your browser, but some Website features may not function.

### 3.2 Analytics cookies

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the Website, which pages are popular, how users move around the Website, and how we can improve our content and services.

Google Analytics may use cookies and similar technologies to collect information such as device information, browser information, approximate location, pages viewed, interactions, and cookie identifiers.

In regions where consent is required, Google Analytics cookies should run only after you give consent.

### 3.3 Affiliate and performance cookies

We use affiliate links and may use Travelpayouts tools, links, widgets, or tracking technologies.

Affiliate cookies and identifiers may help record that you clicked from Originfacts to a travel provider and may help determine whether a commission is payable to us if you later make a booking or purchase.

These cookies may be set by us, Travelpayouts, travel brands, or other third parties. Cookie names, purposes, and durations may vary by provider and campaign.

In regions where consent is required, affiliate or performance tracking cookies should run only after you give consent, unless they are strictly necessary for a service you requested.

### 3.4 Preference cookies

Preference cookies may remember settings such as language, region, currency, display preferences, saved filters, and cookie choices.

### 3.5 Embedded content and third-party cookies

Pages may include embedded tools, links, widgets, maps, images, videos, advertisements, affiliate tools, newsletter forms, or social features from third parties.

Those third parties may set their own cookies when you interact with their content. Their own cookie policies and privacy policies apply.

## 5. Cookie consent

Where required by law, we ask for your consent before using non-essential cookies, including analytics, affiliate, advertising, and similar tracking cookies.

You should be able to:

1. accept non-essential cookies;
2. reject non-essential cookies;
3. choose cookie categories where available; and
4. change your preferences later.

Withdrawing consent will not affect the lawfulness of processing that occurred before withdrawal.

## 6. How to manage cookies

You can manage cookies by:

1. using our cookie banner or cookie preference centre;
2. changing your browser settings to block or delete cookies;
3. using privacy extensions or browser controls;
4. using Google Analytics opt-out tools where available;
5. using device-level privacy settings; and
6. enabling recognised privacy signals such as Global Privacy Control where supported.

Blocking cookies may affect Website functionality, travel widgets, account features, saved preferences, newsletter forms, analytics, and affiliate attribution.

## 7. Do Not Sell or Share / targeted advertising choices

We do not sell personal information for money.

Some privacy laws may treat certain analytics, affiliate, or advertising cookies as "sharing", "targeted advertising", or similar processing. Where applicable, you may opt out through our cookie controls, recognised browser privacy signals where supported, or by emailing support@fxnholdings.com with the subject line "Cookie Opt-Out".

## 8. Third-party websites

When you click a link to a third-party travel provider, affiliate network, booking platform, hotel, airline, car rental provider, or other website, that third party may set its own cookies.

We do not control third-party cookies on third-party websites. Review the cookie policy and privacy policy of the relevant third party before booking or submitting information.
