We use personal information for the following purposes.

### 4.1 To operate the Website

We use information to provide pages, comparison tools, affiliate links, forms, user accounts, reviews, comments, forums, and other Website features.

Lawful basis where UK/EU data protection law applies: performance of a contract, legitimate interests, and, where required, consent.

If you subscribe, we use your email address and preferences to send newsletters, travel tips, promotions, affiliate offers, destination content, and related updates through Mailchimp.

Lawful basis: consent, or another lawful basis where applicable law permits.

We use Google Analytics and similar tools to understand how users interact with the Website, improve content, detect errors, measure performance, and understand travel-resource demand.

Lawful basis: consent where required for analytics cookies and similar technologies; otherwise legitimate interests where lawful.

We use affiliate links, tracking identifiers, referral data, and conversion data to understand whether a user clicked a link and whether a commission may be payable to us.

Lawful basis: consent where required for affiliate tracking cookies or similar technologies; otherwise legitimate interests in operating an affiliate-funded travel information service.

If you post reviews, ratings, comments, photos, forum posts, or travel tips, we process your User Content, account details, metadata, and moderation history to display content, prevent abuse, detect fake reviews, enforce our Terms, handle reports, and maintain trust in the Website.

Lawful basis: performance of a contract, legitimate interests, legal obligation, and, where required, consent.

### 4.7 To prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents

We use technical logs, IP addresses, account data, cookie identifiers, device information, and moderation records to protect the Website, users, and third parties from spam, fraud, fake reviews, cyberattacks, scraping, unlawful content, and misuse.

Lawful basis: legitimate interests and legal obligation.

### 4.8 To comply with law and enforce rights

We may use information to comply with legal obligations, respond to lawful requests, protect rights, enforce our Terms, handle disputes, preserve evidence, and cooperate with regulators or authorities where legally required.

Lawful basis: legal obligation and legitimate interests.
