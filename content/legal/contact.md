## About Originfacts

Originfacts is a travel information and affiliate aggregation website. We provide travel resources, travel tips, comparison content, estimated or live prices, rankings, reviews, ratings, comments, photos, forum posts, and affiliate links for flights, hotels, car rentals, and related travel services.

We do not directly sell travel bookings, take payment for travel bookings, issue tickets, operate airlines, operate hotels, rent vehicles, provide tours, process refunds, or manage cancellations.
